# MVP Scope Card

## Build now

- restricted admin demo with role switch;
- one synthetic source update with area, rent-free, parking, and floor-plan changes;
- junior confirmation and senior publication;
- mobile request extraction from one call and one email fixture;
- current published data lookup and stale-plan block;
- external email draft, attachments, approval, and sandbox send;
- app activity + mock Outlook weekly report;
- five Korean investigation commands;
- building-specific To/Cc recipients;
- audit timeline and source references.

## Do not build now

- production company SSO or MFA;
- real company Outlook tenant access;
- real call-carrier API;
- full OCR for every vendor format;
- all buildings and all weekly report templates;
- autonomous external send;
- native app-store release;
- real company confidential data.
